All About Pikachu

Pikachu are a species of Pokémon, fictional creatures that appear in an assortment of video games, animated television shows and movies, trading card games, and comic books licensed by The Pokémon Company.

Facts:

  • Can evolve into a Raichu
  • Is an electric type
  • Attacks with electricity
  • Ash's first Pokémon

Top 3 things Pikachu love:

  1. Ketchup
  2. Other Pikachu
  3. Ketchup
